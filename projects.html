Completed Projects

Hololens 2 Training Tool

The Hololens 2 Training Application is a comprehensive learning platform designed to provide users with an in-depth understanding of Microsoft's Hololens 2 AR headset.

Developed using Unity, the application offers interactive tutorials, simulations, and guided experiences covering all aspects of the Hololens 2, from basic controls to advanced features.

This project was developed as part of my final year project in NUIG in collaboration with Medtronic. Click the image for more information.

Medtronic Tempe Project

The project leveraged the capabilities of the Hololens AR headset in combination with Scope AR's Worklink software to develop interactive training modules tailored for operators in a Medtronic medical manufacturing facility.

This innovative solution offers hands-on, immersive learning experiences that enhance operator training, improve efficiency, and ensure compliance with industry standards.
